# References & Academic Literature

1. **He, K., Zhang, X., Ren, S., & Sun, J. (2016).**
   *Deep Residual Learning for Image Recognition.* 
   In Proceedings of the IEEE Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR).
   *(Defines the ResNet architecture and residual learning blocks).*

2. **Esteva, A., Kuprel, B., Novoa, R. A., Ko, J., Swetter, S. M., Blau, H. M., & Thrun, S. (2017).**
   *Dermatologist-level classification of skin cancer with deep neural networks.*
   Nature, 542(7639), 115-118.
   *(Pioneering paper showcasing how deep neural networks trained on clinical photos achieve dermatologist-level diagnosis accuracy).*

3. **Tschandl, P., Rosendahl, C., & Kittler, H. (2018).**
   *The HAM10000 dataset, a large collection of multi-source dermatoscopic images of common pigmented skin lesions.*
   Scientific Data, 5, 180161.
   *(Essential reference for public skin condition datasets).*

4. **Kaur, I., & Jakhar, S. (2021).**
   *Computer-aided detection and classification of psoriasis skin lesions using convolutional neural networks.*
   Journal of Medical Systems, 45(4), 48.
   *(Dermatology-specific application of deep learning for erythematous-squamous plaques).*

5. **World Health Organization (2016).**
   *Global report on psoriasis.*
   World Health Organization.
   *(Provides global epidemiology and clinical impact metrics of Psoriasis).*
